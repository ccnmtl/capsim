    PHCX

    Public Health Capstone Experience

    Successful intervention proposals require the application of a systems perspective to approaching complex problems. PHCX lets you practice this application of systems thinking to both public health policy and intervention decision-making which helps students understand which policy interventions, alone and in combination, yield certain potential synergies that will lead to the most effective intervention strategies.

    Simulate

    Decide
